Privacy Policy for Speedway Survival

Last updated: 06/02/2025

At Speedway Survival, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our game.

1. Data We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information. However, our game uses third-party services, such as Google AdMob, which may collect anonymous data to display personalized ads. This data may include:

  • Device unique identifier (Advertising ID).
  • Device type and operating system.
  • Game usage data (e.g., playtime or ad interactions).

2. Use of Data

The data collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Display relevant ads.
  • Improve the game experience.
  • Generate usage statistics.

3. Sharing Data

We do not share personal data with third parties. However, service providers, such as Google AdMob, may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the Google AdMob Privacy Policy for more information.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
